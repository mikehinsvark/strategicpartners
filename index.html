Conversation Framework

Strategic Partner Scripts

Calibrated questions, no-pressure positioning, and curiosity-first framing — designed to open doors without ever feeling like a pitch.

01 — INITIAL PARTNER CONVERSATION
Opening the Door
"Would you be opposed to a simple way to add more value to your business-owner clients — without adding more work or learning a new product?"
"We help business owners uncover hidden money — tax credits, payroll savings, benefit overpayments, energy inefficiencies, and AI optimization. How are your clients currently identifying those kinds of blind spots?"
02 — EXPLORING FIT
Deepening the Conversation
"How do you currently help your clients reduce expenses or increase profitability — beyond your core service?"
"What happens if a client is unknowingly overpaying and no one brings it to their attention?"
"Would it be unreasonable to explore a value-first review that doesn't disrupt your existing relationships?"
03 — THE SOFT INTRO ASK
Making the Ask
"Would you be opposed to making an introduction if we could deliver insights that make you look like the hero — without you having to explain tax code or compliance?"
"If this worked exactly as described, how would that impact your client retention?"
04 — HANDLING HESITATION
Lowering Resistance
"It sounds like protecting your client relationships is important."
"What would need to be true for this to feel like a win for you?"
"How does this compare to other referral partnerships you've seen?"
05 — NEXT STEP CLOSE
Non-Threatening Close
"Would it make sense to test this with one client first?"
"What's the downside of seeing a sample Dreams Score analysis before deciding?"
Power Language

Calibrated phrases that lower resistance and spark curiosity:

• "Help me understand..."
• "How are you currently handling...?"
• "What happens if nothing changes?"
• "Would it be ridiculous to...?"
• "What would need to be true...?"
• "Would you be opposed to...?"
